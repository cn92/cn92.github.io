The Policies

  • Payment will be done via Paypal (USD) only. Work will not start until some form of payment has been made.
    • Payment can be done up-front, or half up-front and half at the end. If it is done half up-front, I will be watermarking the previews until it is fully paid.
    • Paypal email will be provided upon communication.
  • I reserve the right to….
    • Refuse or decline to work with a client.
    • Terminate work on commissioned piece if at any point the client becomes uncomfortable to work with. Payment will be refunded.
    • Refuse artwork with a strict deadline.
    • To record and post commissioned artwork unless requested not to.
    • Utilize private commissions in private portfolios for job applications.
  • Personal information and communication will be kept confidential between the client and artist.
  • All artwork is digital and will be sent as a .png or .jpeg file via email or discord. Box.net or Dropbox might be used if necessary.
  • I will be working on other commissions/artwork at the same time, so if communication or payment is delayed by several days frequently, I will be working on the other commissions in the mean time.
  • All prices are estimates and vary based on complexity.
  • Chargebacks without allowing the artist time to respond (7 business days) will result in the chargeback being challenged and the client being blacklisted.
  • Refunds are not allowed. All sales are final.
  • All artwork requires the signature and is copyright to the artist. Altering/removing the watermark, re-posting and claiming as your’s is strictly prohibited.
  • Do not use for commercial purposes. If you want art for commercial purposes, please speak with me beforehand so that we can work something out and come to an agreement.
  • No re-selling the artwork or editing it and making money off of it.
Please uphold these policies and if you have any further questions, please contact me.
