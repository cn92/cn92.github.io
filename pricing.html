The Process

  • Communication will be done through email or discord, whichever you prefer. However if you see me online/away, that does not mean I’m actually available, so keep in mind there won’t be immediate responses at times.
  • I will normally sketch out a rough-draft and ask for any changes to be made. Please tell me about major changes that you want made before I proceed to the coloring stage.
  • Once we’re past the sketch/brainstorm phase, I’ll move onto the coloring. Feel free to ask for color changes here.
  • After making sure the colors are okay’d, I will begin shading. In the end, I will add some effects and such to make the piece look complete. Minor edits are fine. Anything major will require revision fees based on complexity.
  • Depending on if you paid upfront or did half/half, I will remove the watermarks and leave the art with its signature
